Unlocking a Resilient Future

Introduction

Author: Lutho Tofile

Welcome to my website My name is Lutho Tofile and this is website based on the use of technology by the tourism sector during the pandemic. We know that the tourism industry has been hardly hit by the pandemic due to a large number of infections. The infections are caused by many things including tourism services which were regarded as the main hotspots and spreaders of the virus. The website is a research presentation to shoscase how the tourism sector operated during the pandemic. The aim is find how the sector can use technology for continuose operation and what technologies can be used to promote the safty of traveling.
